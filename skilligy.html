Skilligy

An AI-powered interview intelligence platform that combines Resume Analysis, Speech Recognition, Computer Vision, and Large Language Models to simulate real interview experiences and deliver personalized, actionable feedback for candidates.

PythonFastAPIWhisper ASRMediaPipeOpenCVLLM

Overview

Skilligy is a full-stack AI interview platform developed to help candidates prepare for technical and behavioral interviews through intelligent, real-time evaluation. The application supports Text, Voice, and Video interview modes, leveraging Speech Recognition, Computer Vision, and Large Language Models to analyze communication skills, technical responses, confidence, and engagement. Instead of relying on generic question banks, Skilligy generates personalized interview experiences and provides structured feedback that helps users continuously improve their interview performance.

System Architecture

Skilligy follows a modular architecture where each AI component operates independently while seamlessly integrating through the FastAPI backend. This design improves scalability, simplifies maintenance, and enables efficient processing of multimodal interview data without permanently storing user responses, ensuring better privacy and performance.

  • Frontend Interface: Web-based platform capturing Text, Audio (MediaRecorder API), and Video responses.
  • FastAPI Backend: Handles routing, API integrations, and data processing.
  • AI Processing Modules: Whisper (Speech-to-text), MediaPipe FaceMesh & OpenCV (Visuals), LLM (Evaluation).
  • Scoring Engine: Aggregates evaluation metrics into standardized scores.

Core Modules

1. Resume Analysis & Question Generation

Candidates begin by uploading their resume, which is automatically parsed to extract relevant skills, technologies, education, and experience. Using an LLM, Skilligy generates role-specific interview questions across Technical, HR, and Behavioral categories, creating a personalized interview experience instead of relying on predefined question banks.

2. Text Interview Mode

During text-based interviews, candidate responses are evaluated using a Large Language Model for technical accuracy, relevance, clarity, and logical structure. The platform generates a comprehensive content score alongside personalized strengths and improvement suggestions to help candidates refine future responses.

3. Voice Interview Mode

Voice responses are transcribed using OpenAI Whisper, followed by detailed speech analysis that measures Words Per Minute (WPM), fluency, hesitation, filler words, and speaking confidence. These communication metrics are combined with LLM-based content evaluation to generate a balanced Voice Interview Score and constructive feedback.

4. Video Interview Mode

Video interviews integrate speech transcription with facial behavior analysis using OpenCV and MediaPipe FaceMesh. The system evaluates eye contact, head movement stability, facial engagement, and communication quality before combining these insights into a comprehensive Video Interview Score that emphasizes both technical knowledge and presentation skills.
